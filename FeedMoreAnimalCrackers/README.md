# Feed More Animal Crackers

Lets you feed more animal crackers to animals.  Also works on fish ponds.

## Console Commands
`gellingly.remove_crackers` - Remove all golden crackers from this animal or fish pond.  Only works if the animal menu (menu that opens when you right click on a animal and tells you what their mood is) or fish pond menu (menu that opens if you right click on a fish pond that tells how many fish are in that pond) is open.
